Accessibility

We are committed to making our platform accessible to everyone. Here's how we ensure accessibility:

  • Adherence to WCAG 2.1 guidelines.
  • Use of semantic HTML for proper document structure.
  • Inclusion of alternative text for images.
  • Keyboard navigation support for easy navigation without a mouse.
  • Testing with screen readers to ensure compatibility.

Make Your Experience Better:

  • Use a screen reader for auditory feedback.
  • Adjust text size and contrast in your browser settings.
  • Ensure your browser is up-to-date for optimal performance.
